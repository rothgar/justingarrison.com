## Description

Kubernetes has a lot of components that depend on each other and "scaling" doesn't always mean "up." We'll show how to understand the tradeoffs when you scale, and we'll dive into the technical details of what components become the bottlenecks at large and small scales.

Constraints are unknown until you hit them, and adding more nodes is rarely the right solution. This talk will look at what it takes to find the limits of Kubernetes and what components are replaceable to go beyond the default size recommendations.
